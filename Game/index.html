About GTA5

Grand Theft Auto V, developed by Rockstar North, is an open-world action-adventure game that takes place in the fictional city of Los Santos. As a player, you will control three protagonists with intertwining storylines, experience the vastness of an urban landscape, and immerse yourself in missions, side activities, and intense car chases.

The game also offers a deeply engaging multiplayer experience through GTA Online, where players can join together to complete missions, races, and free-roam adventures.
